About Us

A Legacy of Design Excellence

Founded in 2008, AURA Architecture Studio has grown from a small practice into an award-winning firm with projects spanning residential, commercial, and cultural sectors across the United States.

Our approach combines rigorous attention to detail with a deep respect for context. We believe architecture should respond to its environment, honor its users, and stand as a testament to thoughtful design.

With a team of 35 architects, designers, and planners, we bring diverse perspectives and specialized expertise to every project. Our work has been recognized by the AIA, featured in Architectural Digest, and exhibited at the Venice Biennale.
